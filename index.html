What can KYSS do for you?

Track

Identifying suppliers that have not filed GST is critical to claiming a full input tax credit each month. KYSS connects to GSTN directly and helps you get the filing statuses of all your suppliers at once.

Reconcile

35% of small businesses don't have the means to reconcile their purchases with their Supplier' filings. Purchase Register and GSTR-2A reconciliation just got easier with KYSS. Claim full ITC with KYSS.

Communicate
